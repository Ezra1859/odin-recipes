Oven cooked salmon

Description

Ingredients

  • 500g Salmon fillet/s
  • Half lemon
  • 3 Sweet potatoes
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Add your 3 sweet potatoes into a suitable sized pot
  2. Fill the pot with water past the potatoes and boil on medium-high heat for 30-40 minutes
  3. Season your salmon with salt and pepper
  4. Drizzle your salmon with half a lemon
  5. Place your salmon skin side down onto a tray in your oven
  6. Cook at 180 degrees celsius for 20-30 minutes
  7. Once your potatoes are ready, peel and mash them onto your plate
  8. Take your salmon out of the oven once done and add to plate. Done!
