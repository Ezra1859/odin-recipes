Protein Oats

Description

Ingredients

  • 40-50g oats
  • 1 tbsp. Peanut butter
  • 1x Banana
  • 1x tbsp. Skyr/Greek yogurt
  • Whole fat milk

Steps

  1. Add 40-50g oats to a bowl
  2. Pour the milk up to an inch below the bowl line
  3. Cook in the microwave at max heat for 3 minutes
  4. Remove from microwave, stir, and cook it again for 1 minute
  5. Add 1 tbsp. of peanut butter and stir well
  6. Add 1 tbsp. of yogurt and stir well
  7. Add thin slices of your banana on top and you're done!
